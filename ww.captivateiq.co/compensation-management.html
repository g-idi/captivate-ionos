compensation management

Empower your teams with the right commission plan

Our flexible solution lets you set up any commission plan to motivate the team and crush their quotas.

Build with confidence

Set up any incentive plan using the familiarity and flexibility of spreadsheets, combined with the scale and automation of technology. We built a commission platform designed to help grow your business, not to have you learn another new software.

Get real-time feedback

Our modeling platform allows you to make changes on the fly to any part of your commission plan and see those changes instantaneously. There's no need to wait till you finish building your commission plan before seeing the results.

Avoid costly errors

Easily audit your data and calculations to ensure accuracy and compliance. Our audit logs provide details on every change that was made to your comp plan so there's full traceability and accountability.
